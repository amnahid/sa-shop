# Inventory & Stock Management

The Inventory module is the core of the retail operations, providing a robust catalog and real-time stock tracking across multiple branches.

## Features

### Product Catalog
- **Multi-language Support**: Store product names and descriptions in both English and Arabic.
- **SKU & Barcode Management**: Support for unique SKUs and industry-standard barcodes (EAN, UPC) for quick scanning.
- **Units of Measure**: Support for various units including Piece, KG, Gram, Liter, ML, and Pack.
- **Rich Media**: Attach multiple high-quality images to products with a modern gallery view.
- **Categorization**: Multi-level category hierarchy for better organization and POS navigation.

### Stock Management
- **Multi-branch Tracking**: Real-time stock levels tracked independently for each branch.
- **Low Stock Alerts**: Configurable thresholds per product with visual indicators and system notifications.
- **Stock Movements**: Detailed audit trail for every change (Sale, Purchase, Adjustment, Transfer, Waste).
- **FIFO by Expiry**: Automatically suggests oldest stock batches for sale to minimize waste.

### Grocery Specifics
- **Weight-based Items**: Handle products sold by weight (e.g., meat, vegetables).
- **Expiry Tracking**: Critical tracking for perishable goods with warning systems for items near expiration.

## User Roles
- **Managers**: Can create, edit, and archive products; perform stock adjustments and transfers.
- **Cashiers**: Can view stock levels but cannot modify product data or perform manual adjustments.

## Compliance
- All stock movements are recorded in the audit log.
- VAT rates (Standard 15%, Zero, Exempt) are managed at the product level.
