# Reports & Analytics

Gain deep insights into your business performance with real-time data visualization and detailed financial exports.

## Features

### Sales Performance
- **Dashboard Overview**: Key metrics (Today's Sales, Month-to-Date, Average Order Value) updated in real-time.
- **Top Products**: Identify best-selling items and categories by volume and revenue.
- **Daily Reconciliation**: Track cash vs. card totals and identify shift variances.

### Inventory Reports
- **Low Stock Report**: Immediate list of all items below their safety threshold.
- **Stock Movement Audit**: Complete trail of where every item went (Sold, Expired, Transferred).
- **Expiry Report**: Upcoming expirations prioritized by urgency to facilitate clearance sales.

### Financial & Tax Reports
- **VAT Summary**: Aggregated totals for taxable sales and VAT collected, mapped to ZATCA return boxes.
- **Invoice Export**: Download all invoices in PDF or CSV format for external auditing.
- **Profit & Loss**: High-level view of revenue vs. cost of goods sold (COGS).

### AI Insights (Roadmap)
- **Demand Forecasting**: Predicting stock needs based on historical sales patterns.
- **Anomaly Detection**: Identifying unusual transaction patterns that may indicate fraud or errors.
- **Optimized Reordering**: Suggested purchase orders based on sales velocity and lead times.

## Data Accessibility
- **PDF Export**: Clean, printable reports for meetings and physical filing.
- **CSV Export**: Raw data for advanced analysis in Excel or other tools.
- **Drill-down**: Click on any report metric to see the underlying source transactions.

## User Roles
- **Owners & Accountants**: Full access to all reports and exports.
- **Managers**: Access to inventory and sales reports, but limited access to overall financial P&L.
