# SA Shop Management — Feature Documentation

Welcome to the feature documentation for SA Shop Management, a comprehensive SaaS platform tailored for grocery shops and retail businesses in Saudi Arabia. This system is designed for compliance with ZATCA regulations and focuses on operational efficiency.

## Core Modules

- **[Inventory & Stock Management](./inventory.md)**: Product catalog, category hierarchy, multi-branch stock levels, and expiry tracking.
- **[Point of Sale (POS)](./pos.md)**: Fast checkout, barcode scanning, split payments, and ZATCA Phase 1 compliant receipts.
- **[Accounting & Finance](./accounting.md)**: Invoicing, credit notes (refunds), and automated VAT reporting.
- **[Customers & Suppliers](./customers-suppliers.md)**: CRM for loyalty and supplier management for procurement.
- **[Proposals & Retainers](./proposals-retainers.md)**: Managing business-to-business quotes and recurring service agreements.
- **[HR & Team Management](./hr-team.md)**: Team invitations, role-based access control (RBAC), and basic payroll.
- **[Reports & Analytics](./reports.md)**: Sales performance, stock movements, and financial health insights.
- **[Settings & Administration](./settings.md)**: Business profile, branch configuration, and system preferences.

---

## Technical Foundation

- **ZATCA Compliance**: Phase 1 electronic invoicing (QR codes, UUID, hash chain).
- **Multi-tenancy**: Secure data isolation with tenant-level scoping.
- **Mobile & Tablet Ready**: Responsive design optimized for touch and desktop.
- **Audit Ready**: Comprehensive audit logging for all sensitive operations.
