# Customers & Suppliers

Managing relationships with both your buyers and your providers is streamlined through the CRM and Supplier modules.

## Features

### Customer Management (CRM)
- **Customer Profiles**: Store names (EN/AR), contact details, and VAT registration info for B2B clients.
- **Purchase History**: View all past invoices and returns for any customer.
- **Spending Insights**: Track total lifetime value and visit frequency.
- **Loyalty Integration (Roadmap)**: Points-based systems and tiered rewards for repeat shoppers.
- **Privacy Compliance**: Built-in KSA PDPL consent tracking for marketing and data storage.

### Supplier Management
- **Vendor Profiles**: Keep track of product suppliers, their contact info, and payment terms.
- **Supply Catalog**: Track which products are provided by which suppliers.
- **VAT Verification**: Store supplier VAT numbers for input tax credit claims.

## Procurement (MVP)
- **Manual Stock Receipts**: Attach a supplier to a stock increase adjustment to track source of goods.
- **Purchase Costs**: Record the cost price per batch to calculate accurate COGS (Cost of Goods Sold).

## User Roles
- **Managers**: Full access to create and manage both customers and suppliers.
- **Cashiers**: Can look up customers and associate them with a sale, but cannot create or edit supplier data.

## Compliance
- **PDPL**: All customer data storage includes a timestamped record of consent.
- **VAT**: Supplier VAT numbers are validated against ZATCA formatting rules.
