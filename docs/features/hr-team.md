# HR & Team Management

Efficiently manage your workforce, permissions, and basic payroll compliance within the same platform.

## Features

### Team Management
- **Magic Link Invitations**: Invite team members via email with secure, expiring activation links.
- **Branch Assignment**: Limit cashiers to specific physical branches to prevent unauthorized access.
- **User Profiles**: Manage personal details, contact info, and avatars for all staff.

### Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
- **Cashier**: Optimized for POS operations. Can perform sales and close shifts. Limited access to inventory and no access to accounting reports.
- **Manager**: Can manage inventory, suppliers, and customers. Has authority to void sales and approve discounts.
- **Owner**: Full system access, including business settings, multi-branch configuration, and all financial data.

### Payroll (Roadmap)
- **Wage Protection System (WPS)**: Support for generating SIF files for Mudad/GOSI compliance in KSA.
- **End-of-Service Benefits (EOSB)**: Automated calculation of gratuity according to KSA Labour Law Articles 84-88.
- **Attendance Tracking**: Basic logging of shift start and end times for hourly employees.

## Compliance
- **KSA Labour Law**: Built-in logic for Saudi-specific holiday and gratuity rules.
- **Data Protection**: Staff records are handled according to KSA PDPL requirements.

## User Roles
- **Owners**: Can invite new users and change roles/permissions.
- **Managers**: Can view staff lists and schedules.
